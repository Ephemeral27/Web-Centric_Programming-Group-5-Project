Welcome to Our Web Project

Who We Are

LifeQuest is a personal growth and life-management platform designed to help users take control of their daily lives. We focus on supporting individuals in managing their finances, setting meaningful goals, and tracking progress toward a balanced and successful future. LifeQuest was created as part of a Web Centric Programming course to demonstrate practical web development skills while solving real-world problems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower users to make better life decisions by providing simple, effective tools for money management, goal tracking, and personal organization. LifeQuest aims to encourage positive habits, reduce stress related to finances and planning, and help users stay focused on achieving both short-term and long-term goals.
