What Is This Application?

The Healthy Life & Money Manager is a web-based application designed to help users build better daily habits. It allows users to track their health routines, manage finances, improve productivity, and stay motivated through achievements.

Health habit tracker

Track water intake, sleep, exercise, and meals to build healthier routines.

Expense and budget management

Record your income and expenses to manage your monthly budget.

Productivity and goal tracking

Stay organized with tasks, focus sessions, and productivity tools.

Achievement and reward system

Unlock badges and rewards as you build better habits.

Build Better Habits

Stay consistent and motivated by tracking your progress across multiple areas of your life in one simple platform.
